FaithMelody Short Studio

Import worship audio, add 9:16 visuals, and publish shorts to YouTube, TikTok, Instagram, and Facebook.

What it does

FaithMelody Short Studio is a creator workflow tool for vertical short-form gospel and worship video. Import music from Suno or upload your own audio, pair it with stock or AI visuals, burn in optional lyrics, build a 9:16 MP4, and publish to connected social accounts with one workflow.

Platforms

  • YouTube Shorts — upload via Google OAuth
  • TikTok — direct post via TikTok Login Kit and Content Posting API
  • Instagram Reels — publish via Meta Instagram Graph API
  • Facebook Page Reels — publish via Meta Video API (FaithMelody Page)

TikTok integration

Users connect their TikTok account with Login Kit. Before publishing, the app shows the creator's @username and avatar and requires a manual privacy selection. Videos upload via Direct Post (FILE_UPLOAD). See our Privacy Policy for how OAuth tokens are stored.

Your data

Short Studio runs on your machine or your deployed server. OAuth tokens and media files stay under your control. See our Privacy Policy and Terms of Service for details.
