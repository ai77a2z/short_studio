Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 28, 2026

FaithMelody Short Studio (“we”, “the app”) helps you create and publish short-form worship and gospel videos to social platforms you connect. This policy describes what data the application processes when you use it.

Data we process

  • OAuth tokens — When you connect YouTube, TikTok, or Meta accounts, access and refresh tokens are stored locally in your environment configuration (for example .env.local) on the server where you run the app.
  • Media files — Audio, images, and rendered videos you import or generate are stored in local job folders on that server.
  • Publish metadata — Titles, descriptions, captions, and hashtags you enter for posts.
  • TikTok creator info — When connected, we fetch your TikTok username, avatar, and allowed privacy options to display in the Publish step before you post.
  • Logs — Standard server logs may include API errors and request metadata for debugging.

How we use data

Data is used only to operate the publishing workflow you initiate: building shorts and posting to platforms you authorize. We do not sell personal data.

Third-party services

When you publish, content and metadata are sent to the platforms you select (Google/YouTube, TikTok, Meta/Facebook/Instagram) under their respective terms and privacy policies.

Retention

Job media and tokens remain on your server until you delete them or revoke platform access in TikTok, Meta, or Google settings.

Your choices

You can disconnect OAuth apps in each platform's settings, delete local job folders, and remove tokens from your environment file.
